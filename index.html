TRISTAN NKOGHETN

Social Scientist • PhD Candidate • UX Researcher • Photographer
I enjoy reading, writing, researching, and if i can, take pictures in the process. The stricter the constraints, the more creative the solutions.

This project emerged from an obsession with time, not as a linear progression but rather as an omnipresent force that can both corrupt and liberate. Lorsque le temps est za is a collection of approximations; assembled photographs, prose fragments, and philosophical musings that lead to meditations on temporal experience, identity, and the impossibility of holding onto fleeting moments.
